Application Registration

Application processing Solution

We have a great amount of experience to process applications via Online or Offline and even a conglomerate of both. Online applications are accessible from any location, whereas we can also use the OMR application form in case of the applicant resides in far flung area where internet access is rare. The Online and OMR application form can be customized depending on the requirement of examination body.

SEG SKILLS constructs its technology and resources in an effective manner to provide an end to end Application processing on behalf of the examining body:

Online Application Form

Creating the customized OMR Application form based on the requirement.

Offline OMR Application Form

Designing and delivering web interface in secure environment.

Facility to upload photo and sign

Providing facility to upload applicant photo and signature in Online and Offline Application Form.

Online Payment / Offline payment

Easy integration of either by bank payment gateway or third party payment gateway.

Test center allocation

Facilitating an applicant to select the test centers which ever suits him nearest.

Generate E-admit card

Once the payment process is complete, applicants can easily generate their e-admit card with a unique application number.

Data Accessibility

Two separate logins interface accessibility, one for the applicant to upload the information and other for the authorized executive to check the data as prescribed by them.

Generate Multiple Reports

The system would be generating reports, applicants can also be shortlisted based on the parameters.
